WELCOME TO THE GREAT NECK NORTH ASTRONOMY CLUB WEBSITE

Founded by current President Christopher Yang, the Astronomy Club strives to engage students with an interactive course, providing students with the opportunity to learn astronomy and astrophysics. We aim to spread knowledge through lectures and events, while instilling passion of the subject into our members. Within the club’s first year of opening, we had already amassed over 25 members. This year, we will continue to gather more students and improve our curriculum.

OUR GOALS

  • Learn Astronomy

    • Our main goal is to teach astronomy to members, through interactive slides and lectures. This year, we will hold lessons over ZOOM. We also provide a list of external resources to further help members. Although we expect members to pay attention, we also host a welcoming environment and prioritize having fun!


  • Participate in Astronomy Events

    • We plan to organize astronomy-related field trips and events, such as stargazing, taking place at the Great Neck North field.


  • Prepare for the USAAAO

    • Our curriculum is based on the USA Astronomy and Astrophysics Olympiads (USAAAO). Although optional, students are encouraged to take this olympiad to solidify their knowledge and to potentially participate in the National Astronomy Competition (NAC).
