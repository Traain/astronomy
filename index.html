WELCOME TO THE GREAT NECK NORTH ASTRONOMY CLUB WEBSITE

Founded by current Astronomy Club President Christopher Yang, the Astronomy Club strives to engage students with an interactive course, providing students with the opportunity to learn astronomy. OUR GOALS

  • Learn Astronomy

    • Our main goal is to teach astronomy to members, through interactive slides and lectures. We also provide a list of external resources to further help members. Although we expect members to pay attention, we also host a welcoming environment and prioritize having fun!


  • Participate in Astronomy Events

    • We plan to organize astronomy-related events, such as stargazing, taking place at the Great Neck North field.


  • Prepare for the USAAAO

    • Our curriculum is based on the USA Astronomy and Astrophysics Olympiads (USAAAO). Although optional, students are encouraged to take this olympiad to solidify their knowledge and to potentially participate in the National Astronomy Competition (NAC).
